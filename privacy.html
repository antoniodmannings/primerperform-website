Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Primer Performance ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") operates primerperform.com. This Privacy Policy governs how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard personal information from visitors and clients.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Contact Forms: Name, email, phone number, organization, inquiry topic
  • Email Communications: Correspondence with info@primerperform.com
  • Consulting Engagements: Project-specific data as defined in engagement agreements
  • Training Registrations: Participant information, background, credentials

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Server Logs: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time and date stamps
  • Analytics: User behavior, session duration, traffic sources (via third-party services)
  • Cookies: Session and preference information (see Section 5)

2.3 Information From Third Parties

We may receive information about you from publicly available business records, professional directories, or referral sources.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal information under the following legal bases:

  • Consent: Where you have explicitly consented
  • Contractual Performance: To fulfill consulting or training agreements
  • Legitimate Interest: Business operations, security, and legal compliance
  • Legal Obligation: Compliance with laws and regulations

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Responding to inquiries and providing services
  • Delivering training and consulting engagements
  • Improving website functionality and user experience
  • Sending updates, newsletters, or promotional materials (with consent)
  • Maintaining security, detecting fraud, and preventing unauthorized access
  • Fulfilling legal or contractual obligations

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to enhance user experience and analyze site performance. You may disable cookies through browser settings; however, some features may become unavailable.

5.1 Cookie Types

  • Essential: Required for site functionality
  • Analytics: Google Analytics (non-identifying traffic data)
  • Preference: Language, layout, and user preferences

6. Third-Party Service Providers

We may share information with service providers who assist in our operations (hosting, analytics, email delivery). These partners are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for specified purposes.

7. Data Retention

  • Contact Inquiries: Retained for 2 years unless you request deletion
  • Client Data: Retained per engagement agreement or 3 years, whichever is longer
  • Analytics: Aggregated data retained indefinitely; individual identifiers deleted after 26 months

8. International Data Transfers

Our servers are located in the United States. If you are located outside the US and provide information, you consent to the transfer of that information to the US and its processing under US law and this policy.

9. Your Privacy Rights

9.1 Access and Portability

You may request a copy of the personal information we maintain about you in a portable format.

9.2 Correction

You may request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete information.

9.3 Deletion

You may request deletion of personal information, subject to legal retention obligations.

9.4 Opt-Out

You may opt out of promotional communications by clicking "unsubscribe" in our emails or contacting us directly.

9.5 California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know, delete, and opt-out of sale of personal information. Contact us to exercise these rights.

To exercise any privacy right, contact: info@primerperform.com

10. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no security system is impenetrable. We cannot guarantee absolute security but maintain reasonable safeguards against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

11. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information and notify the parent or guardian.

12. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. This Privacy Policy applies only to Primer Performance. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites and encourage you to review their policies.

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes are effective immediately upon posting. Continued use of our website constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
