Module Catalog

Five Categories. Fifteen Modules.

Each module is standalone or stackable. Custom sequencing available for multi-day engagements.

Leadership Foundations

Adaptive Leadership Under Pressure

Decision-making frameworks for ambiguous, high-stakes environments. OODA loop application with structured After Action Review.

Values-Based Leadership

Aligning personal and organizational values with observable leadership behavior. Self-assessment, peer feedback, and commitment protocol.

Leader as Coach

Shifting from directive to developmental leadership. Questioning techniques, active listening, and the Hold-Hug-Huddle framework.

Communication & Conflict

High-Stakes Communication

Structured communication under pressure. Message discipline, audience analysis, and delivery calibration for difficult conversations.

Conflict Resolution & De-Escalation

Interest-based negotiation, emotional regulation, and verbal de-escalation techniques drawn from law enforcement and military doctrine.

Feedback as a Leadership Tool

Giving and receiving feedback that produces behavior change. SALUTE observation-to-action framework applied to professional development.

Resilience & Stress Management

Sustained Performance Under Pressure

STEADY methodology for maintaining cognitive function and decision quality during extended stress. Physiological and psychological anchoring.

Recovery & Reset

Post-crisis recovery protocols. Identifying performance degradation signals and implementing structured reset sequences.

Emotional Intelligence for Leaders

Self-awareness, self-regulation, social awareness, and relationship management. Applied EQ assessment with behavioral anchoring exercises.

Team Dynamics & Decision-Making

Team Cohesion & Trust Building

GARDEN experiential cycle applied to team formation. Structured exercises in vulnerability, interdependence, and collective accountability.

Crisis Decision-Making

Rapid assessment, resource allocation, and delegation under time pressure. Scenario-based exercises with real-time consequence feedback.

Cross-Functional Collaboration

Breaking silos through structured interdependence. Role rotation, shared accountability models, and inter-team communication protocols.

Applied Leadership (Capstone)

Leadership Integration Capstone

Full-day synthesis exercise integrating all prior modules. Scenario-based assessment with structured debrief and individual development plan.

After Action Review Facilitation

Training leaders to conduct effective AARs within their own teams. Facilitation technique, question architecture, and follow-through protocols.

30-60-90 Day Transfer Plan

Structured behavior transfer planning with accountability checkpoints. Ensures workshop learning converts to sustained workplace behavior change.
