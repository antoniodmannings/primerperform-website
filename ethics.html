Our Commitment to Integrity

Primer Performance operates under a foundational commitment to ethical practice, transparency, and professional integrity. Our methodology of "Integration Over Isolation™" extends beyond training delivery to encompass how we conduct business, treat clients, and serve our community.

Core Values

Integrity and Honesty

We communicate truthfully about our capabilities, limitations, and the realistic outcomes clients should expect from our services. We do not oversell or misrepresent our expertise. Our word is our bond.

Client-Centered Excellence

We prioritize client objectives and outcomes above all else. Our consulting and training are tailored to your specific context, goals, and constraints—not a one-size-fits-all approach. We measure success by your success.

Respect for the Individual

We respect the dignity, autonomy, and diverse backgrounds of every participant and stakeholder. We recognize that effective leadership and performance require honoring the full humanity of the people we serve.

Continuous Learning

We hold ourselves accountable to ongoing professional development, evidence-based practices, and honest feedback about our impact. We acknowledge what we don't know and commit to improvement.

Confidentiality and Trust

Client information, proprietary data, and sensitive disclosures shared during engagements are protected with the highest standard of confidentiality. Trust is earned through consistent protection of vulnerable information.

Responsible Innovation

We innovate thoughtfully. New methodologies, technologies, and approaches are implemented only after rigorous evaluation and transparent communication with clients about potential risks and limitations.

Ethical Guidelines for Specific Services

Training and Instruction

  • Safety First: All training prioritizes participant safety and complies with applicable laws and regulations
  • No Coercion: Participation in training is voluntary. Participants can withdraw without penalty
  • Transparent Curriculum: We clearly communicate learning objectives, methodologies, assessment criteria, and potential risks before training begins
  • Qualified Instructors: All instructors hold current certifications and liability insurance

Consulting Services

  • Conflict of Interest Disclosure: We disclose any potential conflicts and recuse ourselves when appropriate
  • Independence: Our recommendations are based on your needs, not on our financial incentives
  • Deliverable Clarity: Engagement agreements clearly specify deliverables, timelines, and success metrics
  • Honest Assessment: We provide candid feedback about organizational readiness, gaps, and realistic timelines for change

Intellectual Property

  • We respect client intellectual property and do not repurpose client work without explicit permission
  • Our proprietary methodologies ("Integration Over Isolation™") are protected and not shared with competing organizations
  • We attribute sources and respect copyright and intellectual property rights of others

Limitations and Disclaimers

Scope of Expertise

While our services span leadership development, tactical training, and consulting, we acknowledge limitations in our expertise. We do not provide legal advice, medical guidance, or psychological treatment. We refer clients to appropriate professionals when matters fall outside our scope.

Results Variability

Training and consulting outcomes depend on many factors including client readiness, implementation fidelity, organizational culture, and external conditions. We cannot guarantee specific results and are transparent about this reality.

Regulatory Compliance

Clients are responsible for ensuring that their use of our training and consulting aligns with applicable laws, regulations, and organizational policies. We provide guidance but cannot serve as legal counsel.

Commitment to Continuous Improvement

These ethical guidelines are reviewed annually and updated as our practice evolves. We solicit client feedback, participate in professional development, and commit to holding ourselves accountable to the highest standards of integrity and excellence.
