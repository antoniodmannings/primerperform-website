Accessibility Commitment

Primer Performance is committed to ensuring that our website is accessible to all visitors, including individuals with disabilities. We strive to comply with Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards.

Conformance Status

This website is designed to meet WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards. We regularly audit our content and technical implementation to maintain compliance and identify areas for improvement.

Accessibility Features

  • Semantic HTML: Content uses proper heading hierarchy and semantic elements for screen reader compatibility
  • Color Contrast: Text meets WCAG AA color contrast requirements (minimum 4.5:1 for normal text)
  • Keyboard Navigation: All functionality is accessible via keyboard alone
  • Responsive Design: Content adapts to different screen sizes and zoom levels (up to 200%)
  • Form Labels: All form fields have descriptive labels associated with inputs
  • Alternative Text: Images include appropriate alt text descriptions
  • Focus Indicators: Interactive elements show visible focus states
  • Language Declaration: Page language is declared for assistive technologies

Screen Reader Support

This website is compatible with popular screen readers including:

  • NVDA (Windows)
  • JAWS (Windows)
  • VoiceOver (Mac/iOS)
  • TalkBack (Android)

Browser and Assistive Technology Support

This website is regularly tested with:

  • Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge browsers
  • NVDA and JAWS screen readers
  • Keyboard-only navigation
  • Zoom up to 200%

Known Limitations

We are aware of the following accessibility challenges and are working to address them:

  • PDF Documents: Downloadable PDFs may not be fully accessible. Please contact us for accessible alternatives.
  • Embedded Content: Third-party embedded content (videos, maps) may have limited accessibility features.
  • Legacy Content: Older pages or archived documents may not fully meet current standards.
